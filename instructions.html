The website opens to a clean game with both sides starting at zero. Clicking the "+" or "-" will add and subract points from the board, do not subtract points when the opposite team scores a point, the website will do this automatically. Clicking "End Round" will automatically find the total points earned and credit it to the appropriate team. If a team scores more than 21 point clicking "Reset to 15" will restore the score and return the points on the board to zero. Clicking "New Game" will reset all point values and scores to zero, when a team wins the website will also reset to zero for a new game.
